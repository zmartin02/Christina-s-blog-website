                                    Start reading...         Holding Up the Universe

    Books have a way of making you think a little more. Within the bounds, there is an answer for anything. All of my passion for writing I owe to reading. Books have shaped me and helped me grow to be who I am.

    Sometimes reading brought me new worlds, worlds of magic and escapism. Sometimes reading took me into the consciousness of a character overcoming battles, making me stronger. I have lived so many lives through books and learned so many lessons from the characters. Books have always been such a big part of my life. Here, on this blog, I would like to update all of my recent reading, all of the books that make me love reading as much as I did as a kid. Books that I cannot put down.
