If I asked you to read no further would you listen?

There is something so enticing about reading a journal and something so private about writing one. I would like to say this section will consist of “my deepest thoughts” or something like that, but honestly, it won’t. I will subconsciously filter my words with the knowledge of public perception.

But, I promise I will try and be honest.

Or at least, one day I will. I have to get used to this first.

There is something so daunting about your work on the internet. Even if nobody reads it.
